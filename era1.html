Short History Regarding Tea

Ancient tea origins

Tea was first recorded in China more than 4,000 years ago, beginning as a medicinal drink before growing into a daily tradition.

Silk Road tea trade

The Silk Road carried tea westward, making it a prized commodity that linked Asian and European cultures.

British tea rituals

By the 17th century, tea had become central to British life, inspiring afternoon tea and global trade rivalries.
